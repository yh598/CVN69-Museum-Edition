# Milestone 5 frozen-period configuration audit

Configuration window: **14 October 2023 through 14 July 2024**. Production geometry is a new parametric, 1:700, FDM-safe visual reconstruction. Public photographs establish type, major silhouette, and visible folding state; they do not establish controlled lofts, exact parking coordinates, or insignia artwork.

## Evidence and accuracy classes

- **Dimensionally supported:** official full-scale overall length/span/rotor/dome dimensions and the approved 476 mm ship/deck datums.
- **Photo supported:** major planform family, single/two-seat canopy distinction, Growler wingtip pods, Hawkeye dome/four-fin tail, C-2 cargo fuselage, helicopter rotor-fold state.
- **Engineering representation:** folded-span envelopes, printable keel/gear bridge, minimum-thickness fins/rotors, neutral raised squadron ID tiles, and exact parking coordinates.
- **Excluded:** weapons loads, open panels, individual bureau numbers, unverified mission pods, fine stencil art, exact squadron insignia, E-2D, CMV-22B, F-35C, and any aircraft not evidenced in the frozen window.

All raised identity pieces use the confirmed squadron names/tail-code context but deliberately avoid invented insignia artwork. Material-color accents are functional object separation, not a claim of exact deployment paint.
