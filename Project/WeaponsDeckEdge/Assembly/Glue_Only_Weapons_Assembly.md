# Glue-Only Defensive Systems and Deck-Edge Assembly

## Before assembly

1. Print and test `Weapon_Mount_Interface_Test_Coupon.3mf` before committing production parts. The fit is 0.25 mm per side; dry insertion should seat without forcing and should not rock.
2. Confirm every STL was printed in its exported z=0 orientation. Use 0.12 mm layers for launchers, CIWS parts, faces, railings, ladders, and lights; use 0.16 mm for sponsons, the boat platform, boat, cradle, davit, and coupon.
3. Dry-fit every keyed foundation to the identically named sponson. The asymmetric chamfer is the forward/outboard orientation key; never sand it into symmetry.
4. Use only small amounts of medium CA. Keep glue out of socket bottoms so the 1.20 mm seating depth remains controlled.

## Assembly sequence

1. Glue each additive sponson/platform to the documented deck-edge location. The open glue channel faces the approved ship-side edge and remains hidden. Do not cut, drill, or overwrite the approved deck or hull.
2. Seat each weapon foundation into its matching 0.25 mm-per-side top socket. Apply CA to the open channel/vertical lands, then hold flat until cured. Installed X/Y/Z seating error is designed as 0.00 mm and must remain under 0.10 mm; visible lean must remain under 0.30°.
3. Glue each launcher or CIWS upper body into the shallow asymmetric socket in its foundation.
4. Add dark launcher faces to the outboard side of RAM and Sea Sparrow bodies. The raised grid is an enlarged FDM-safe display treatment, not an exact cell model.
5. Add each Ivory White CIWS dome and Silk Silver barrel. The barrel is a fixed 1.00 mm-diameter representation and does not move.
6. Install life-raft groups on the documented port/starboard deck-edge runs. The Ivory White backing faces the ship and the canisters project outboard.
7. Assemble the port utility boat in this order: lower access platform, cradle, Ash Gray boat, Charcoal insert, then davit. The boat is a generic photo-informed representation; do not reinterpret it as a verified RHIB subtype.
8. Add railings, ladders, navigation-light housings, and emergency-locker groups last to reduce handling damage.

## Handling and inspection

- Do not force a keyed part or reverse an installation. Reprint the coupon or mating component if the fit is too tight.
- Reject any platform with a warped seating plane or any weapon with more than 0.30° visible lean.
- Keep all deck elevators, catapult tracks, arresting wires, raised markings, the twelve approved landing pads, and the island interface unobstructed.
- The model contains no functional aiming, firing, loading, or internal weapon mechanism.
